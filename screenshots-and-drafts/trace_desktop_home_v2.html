Most city guides tell you where to eat.
TRACE tells you what to look at.

A curated creative exploration platform mapping Toronto’s hidden visual culture — ghost signs, brutalist architecture, public art, murals, and typographic landmarks that most people walk past without noticing. Built by a designer, for designers.
